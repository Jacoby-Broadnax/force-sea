Sea Force is a novel approach to career exploration in Harrison County, Mississippi involving partnerships with local high schools and businesses to match students with the career they think they want.Through a grant from the Workforce Investment Board, juniors and seniors who are recommended by the school are then interviewed by their choice of business. In turn, the Harrison County Development Commission pays the student while the employer gets a talented employee at no cost.
